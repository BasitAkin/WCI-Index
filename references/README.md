# Source Documents

This catalogue lists every document from which input data for the WCI analysis were curated. Documents are grouped by the type of data they provide.

## Site-Level Water Withdrawal and Consumption Data

### Google LLC (2025). *2025 Environmental Report.*
- **URL:** https://sustainability.google/reports/google-2025-environmental-report/
- **Pages:** 110-111
- **Sites covered:** Council Bluffs (IA), Mayes County (OK), The Dalles (OR), Douglas County (GA), Midlothian (TX), Henderson (NV), Botetourt County (VA)
- **Data extracted:** Site-level annual water withdrawal (W), consumption (C), consumptive ratio (r = C/W), PUE
- **Assurance:** Externally assured by Ernst & Young (EY)

### Han, M. et al. (2026). "Small Bottle, Big Pipe: Quantifying and Addressing the Impact of Data Centres on Public Water Systems."
- **Sites covered:** Lebanon (IN), Wisconsin Site (WI)
- **Data extracted:** Facility-level water demand (W), peaking factor (PF), consumptive ratio range (r), national projections
- **Notes:** Provides industry-average r = 0.77 (Table 2, hyperscale weighted average)

### Microsoft Corporation (2025). *2024 Environmental Sustainability Report.*
- **URL:** https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/corporate-responsibility/sustainability/report
- **Page:** 37
- **Data extracted:** Fleet-level water withdrawal 2022-2024 (basis for 24% CAGR HIGH growth scenario)

## Growth Scenario Sources

### Shehabi, A. et al. (2024). *2024 United States Data Center Energy Usage Report.* LBNL-2001637.
- **URL:** https://eta.lbl.gov/publications/2024-lbnl-data-center-energy-usage-report
- **DOI:** 10.71468/P1WC7Q
- **Data:** US national data-centre water growth rate 2014-2023 (basis for 13% CAGR MODERATE scenario)

### EPRI (2026). *Powering Intelligence: Analyzing Artificial Intelligence and Data Center Energy Consumption.*
- **URL:** https://powering-intelligence.epri.com
- **Data:** US DC electricity 380-790 TWh by 2030 (9-17% of total); cited in manuscript growth discussion

### EIA (2026). *Short-Term Energy Outlook, April 2026.*
- **URL:** https://www.eia.gov/outlooks/steo/
- **Data:** Total US electricity demand 4,108-4,244 BkWh (2026-2027); growth 1.2-3.3%

### ICPRB (2026). *Data Centers and Water Use in the Potomac River Basin.*
- **URL:** https://www.potomacriver.org/publications/data-centers-water-use-potomac/
- **Data:** 290+ facilities, 4 MGD avg / 15 MGD peak consumptive use; 9-12% of regional consumptive use
